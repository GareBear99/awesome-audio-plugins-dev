# Issues for Reevaluation

This page tracks **closed issues tied to declined entries** that may deserve another review later.

The goal is to avoid losing potentially strong projects just because they were not ready at the time of first review.

Related pages:
- [DECLINED.md](DECLINED.md)
- [REEVALUATION.md](REEVALUATION.md)
- [BANNED.md](BANNED.md)

---

## What Goes Here

Use this page for:

- closed issues related to declined submissions
- entries whose maintainers returned with updates
- cases where the first review was correct, but the project later improved
- issues worth revisiting after docs, screenshots, links, downloads, or trust signals were fixed

Do **not** use this page for permanently banned entries.

---

## Review Status Labels

- **Watch** — not ready yet, but may be worth checking later
- **Needs Proof** — claims changed, but still lacks enough evidence
- **Ready to Recheck** — enough changed to justify a fresh review
- **Resolved** — reevaluated and moved to approved or declined again

- **Original Decline Reason:** Short summary

- **Latest Notes:** Short summary
- **Final Result:** Pending / Approved / Declined Again / Moved to Banned

---

## Suggested Process

1. Link the closed issue here.
2. State the original decline reason clearly.
3. Define exactly what would justify a reevaluation.
4. Reopen the decision only when the evidence changes, not just because time passed.
