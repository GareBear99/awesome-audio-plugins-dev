# XyloCore

> **TizWildin Entertainment HUB — Instruments**
> **Role:** Playable xylophone / tuned-percussion instrument
> **Status:** ⚠️ Beta
> **Formats:** VST3 · AU
> **License:** FREE (open source)

## Tagline
A playable, velocity-aware xylophone and tuned-percussion instrument designed to slot into the Instrudio family without its overhead.

## Overview
XyloCore is the focused, tuned-percussion entry point in the TizWildin instrument line. Where Instrudio bundles ten instruments under one shell, XyloCore exposes a single, high-quality tuned-percussion engine optimized for quick MIDI sketching and layering.

It is ideal for producers who want a clean, believable xylophone / mallet voice without loading a full instrument suite.

## Core features
- Velocity-mapped sample playback across a full playable range
- Keyboard-based MIDI control with consistent tuning reference
- Low-footprint load for quick insertion into any template
- Cleanly scoped scope so the instrument can be extended later

## Typical workflows
- Arpeggiated mallet lines in cinematic and game scoring
- Bright rhythmic layers in pop and hybrid productions
- Teaching / learning context for tuned percussion parts

## Compatibility
macOS (Intel + Apple Silicon), Windows 10+
