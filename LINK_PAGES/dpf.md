This page gives readers a cleaner handoff from the main list to deeper evaluation. Instead of forcing a blind click, it explains what **DPF** is, what kind of reader it suits, and where to go next.

- Worth comparing by API coverage, docs, build tooling, GUI flexibility, and long-term maintenance.
- Strong frameworks reduce friction without boxing in advanced work.
