# Declined

This page tracks submissions, packages, frameworks, resources, or projects that were **reviewed and declined** for inclusion in this awesome list.

A decline here does **not always mean permanent rejection**. Some entries are declined because they are incomplete, inactive, unclear, off-scope, or need stronger proof before they fit the standards of this repository.

For entries that may become eligible later, see [REEVALUATION.md](REEVALUATION.md).
For permanent removals or hard disqualifications, see [BANNED.md](BANNED.md).

---

## Decline Reasons Used On This Repo

- **Off-scope** — not meaningfully related to free audio plugins, DSP tools, plugin frameworks, or relevant audio resources.
- **Too little information** — unclear website/repo, weak documentation, missing screenshots, or not enough details to judge.
- **Not actually usable yet** — concept page, placeholder, broken download, or no real public access.
- **Low trust signals** — suspicious packaging, vague claims, no author identity, no docs, or poor presentation.
- **Not free** — paywalled, demo-only, or misleadingly presented as free.
- **Duplicate / redundant** — already represented in the list or too close to an existing entry.
- **Inactive / abandoned** — appears dead, unavailable, or no longer maintained enough to recommend.
- **Quality threshold not met** — does not currently meet the curation standard of this repo.
