## Why These Packs Matter

These packs are useful because they are:
- structured around actual production workflows
- genre-focused instead of random file dumps
- connected to a wider plugin and DSP ecosystem
- easier to evaluate than anonymous pack pages with weak documentation

## What Makes a Good Sample Pack

- clear organization
- accurate naming
- usable one-shots and loops
- genre or workflow focus
- enough documentation to know what is included
- predictable file structure
